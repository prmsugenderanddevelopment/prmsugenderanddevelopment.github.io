Anti-Sexual Harrasment

CHED Memorandum Order No. 1, Series of 2015 or Establishing the Guidelines in Gender and Development in Higher Education Institutions; Republic Act 11313 or the Safe Spaces Act, and Article 24, Part VII of the 2017 University GAD Code mandate for the University to ensure that necessary policies and mechanisms are in place to prevent and punish sexual harassment and other related sexual offenses.
